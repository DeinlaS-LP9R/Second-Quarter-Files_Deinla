The Republic of the Philippines, composed of 7,641 beautiful, welcoming and hospitable islands, is the premiere vacation paradise for any person or family looking for a wonderful place to relax in. From white beaches to bustling cities to calm forests, the Philippines has a place for everyone.

Here are the top destinations.

Love, the Philippines!


PUERTO PRINCESA

Home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Puerto Princesa Underground River, Puerto Princesa is a famous tourist destination
on Palawan.

BAGUIO CITY

Sitting in the mountains in central Luzon, Baguio is a chilly, windy place of exploration and relaxation
for people from the capital willing to trek up north.

BORACAY

Boracay's world-famous white beaches were rehabilitated recently, making the place an even greater
tourist destination for those seeking warm, sunny beaches.

BOHOL

Home to the famous and stunning Chocolate Hills, Bohol Island and the nearby Cebu are a great southern destination
for families.
